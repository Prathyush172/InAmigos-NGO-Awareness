About the Foundation

Building change through organized service.

Founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation brings volunteers, interns, partners, and community members together around practical social work. Its programs focus on people and causes that often need sustained support rather than short-term attention.

The foundation combines awareness campaigns with on-ground initiatives, helping young people understand social responsibility while contributing to education, care, dignity, and environmental action.

Mission & Vision

A clearer path for service-led development.

Mission Preview

To mobilize people, skills, and resources for meaningful social initiatives that improve access to learning, food, safety, dignity, and opportunity for communities in need.

Vision Preview

To create an India where every person can participate in social change, every child can learn, every woman can grow with independence, and every life is treated with care.

Why Volunteer With Us

Service that teaches, connects, and creates visible change.

Volunteers at InAmigos Foundation get exposure to real community work while developing leadership, communication, teamwork, and campaign planning skills. The work is meaningful because it connects awareness with practical participation.

Whether someone can teach, design, coordinate, research, document, organize drives, or simply show up consistently, the foundation creates space for people to contribute responsibly.
