About Us

Organized compassion, led by people who believe change can be shared.

InAmigos Foundation brings together citizens, students, professionals, volunteers, and partners who want their time and talent to serve a wider purpose.

Our Story

A foundation created for practical community action.

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla with the belief that social change becomes stronger when people work together with clarity and consistency. From Chhattisgarh, the foundation has grown as a platform for awareness, volunteering, internships, campaigns, and community support initiatives.

The name reflects friendship, participation, and collective responsibility. Rather than focusing on a single social issue, the foundation works across connected areas of need: education, food security, women empowerment, animal welfare, environment, and skill development.

Mission

To create accessible opportunities for service and awareness by connecting volunteers, interns, donors, and partners with programs that improve lives through education, care, relief, skill-building, and responsible citizenship.

Vision

To build a compassionate society where every child can learn, every person can live with dignity, women and youth can become self-reliant, animals are protected, and communities participate in safeguarding nature.

Founder

Mr. Govind Shukla

Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder and CEO of InAmigos Foundation, established the organization to give structured direction to service-minded individuals. His leadership focuses on turning public concern into organized initiatives that can involve young people, communities, and institutional partners.

Under his guidance, the foundation emphasizes transparency, participation, and impact-oriented programs that allow volunteers to learn while serving.
