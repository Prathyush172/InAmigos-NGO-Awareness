Get Involved

Your time, skills, and support can become someone else’s opportunity.

InAmigos Foundation welcomes people who want to contribute thoughtfully, learn from community work, and help campaigns reach more lives.

Volunteer

Become part of field activities, awareness campaigns, donation drives, teaching support, and project coordination. Volunteering is ideal for people who want to give consistent time and learn through real community engagement.

Start Volunteering

Become an Intern

Internships offer structured exposure to social work, campaign planning, content creation, research, documentation, outreach, and coordination. Interns can develop professional skills while contributing to public welfare.

Apply Interest

Partner With InAmigos

Institutions, colleges, companies, and community groups can collaborate on CSR initiatives, awareness programs, skill sessions, and local drives that align with shared social responsibility goals.

Discuss Partnership

Support the NGO

Support can include resources, campaign materials, professional expertise, local connections, awareness sharing, or financial assistance for approved charitable work and project needs.

What You Gain

Purposeful experience with real-world value.

Working with an NGO helps participants understand community realities, develop empathy, improve communication, and practice leadership. The experience is valuable because it is rooted in responsibility rather than performance alone.

Every volunteer, intern, and partner adds strength to the foundation’s ability to reach communities with consistency and care.

Join the mission and help make service more visible, organized, and accessible.
